What is CS:GO?

CS:GO or rather Counter Strike: Global Offensive is a first person shooter game. It's played by millions of players each day. It's a teamwork based game and it requires two teams to play on. Counter Terrorist is one of the team in the game and Terrorist is another. These two teams would have to defend or attack from the other side and whichever achieve this goal wins.
